Apple's UI Over the Years

1984 - First UI Macintosh Era

The original Macintosh introduced a graphical user interface (GUI) and a desktop metaphor.

1998 - iMac & Mac OS X Era

With iMac and Mac OS X, Apple embraced a colorful and elegant design approach.

2001 - iPod & iTunes Era

Apple's iPod and iTunes introduced a clean and minimalistic design aesthetic.

2007 - iPhone & iOS Era

The iPhone revolutionized the smartphone industry and introduced the iOS operating system.

2013 - iOS 7 & iOS 8 Era

iOS 7 marked a significant design shift with a cleaner and flatter aesthetic.

2018 - iPhone X & iOS 11 Era

With iPhone X and iOS 11, Apple embraced edge-to-edge displays and refined the UI design.

2021 - M1 Macs & macOS Big Sur Era

Apple's M1 Macs and macOS Big Sur introduced a unified design language across devices.

2022 - iOS 15 & iPadOS 15 Era

iOS 15 and iPadOS 15 brought enhanced widgets, multitasking, and redesigned notifications.

2023 - Silicon & macOS Monterey Era

Apple Silicon and macOS Monterey introduced powerful hardware and a refreshed UI experience.

2024 - AirTags & Find My Era

With AirTags and improvements to the Find My app, Apple enabled users to easily locate their belongings.

2026 - AR Glasses & ARKit Era

Apple's AR Glasses and advancements in ARKit technology opened up new possibilities for augmented reality experiences.
